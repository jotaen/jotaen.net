My name is Jan, I live in Konstanz (Germany) and work as a software developer. In this blog I share thoughts about creating things with computers.

I’m interested in the entire stack of web development from devops via backend architecture upwards to digging pixels. I’m not a tech geek in the first place; my biggest driver is creating great software products that are meaningful for the people who use them. I like deliberate structures and clear designs – be it code or layout – and am a typography enthusiast alongside.

As counterbalance, I love to spend my spare time in the mountains: I am a passionate climber and hiker; besides, I enjoy to explore hidden valleys by motorcycle.
