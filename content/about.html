My name is Jan, I live in Berlin and work as a software developer. In this blog I share thoughts about creating things with computers.

I’m interested in the entire stack of web development from devops via backend architecture upwards to digging pixels and tweaking user interfaces. I’m not a tech geek in the first place; my biggest driver is to create great software products that are meaningful for the people who use them. I like deliberate structures and clear designs – be it code or layout – and am a typography enthusiast alongside.

In my spare time I am a passionate alpinist and love to climb in the mountains. Besides, I do other fun stuff like riding motorcycle, longboarding or making music.
