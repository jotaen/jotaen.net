Hi, my name is Jan. I live in Berlin and work as software developer.

My primary driver is to create great software products that are meaninful for the people who use them. I’m passionate about technological visions and the focus of my architectural thinking is to gradually establish sustainable structures that facilitate agile, iterative product development in the long run. On the technical side, my interests span the entire stack from the bare metal all the way up to the user interface. I like clear and deliberate designs – be it systems, code or layout – and am a typography enthusiast alongside.

Besides work, I love climbing in the mountains, travelling around on my motorbike or playing the piano. On the background picture, you see me on top of the summit needle of Salbitschijen in Switzerland.
